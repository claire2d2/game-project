Bool you can eat hotpot

Welcome to a game of Snake ... for the foodies!
Here you will not play a snake, but someone at a all you can eat hotpot where the objective, as in every all you can eat restaurant, is to stuff yourself with the most foods possible and get your money's worth!
The game will be over if :
  • you leave the restaurant (aka touch the game borders)
  • you run over your pile of plates
  • pick coriander because that is the devil's herb
